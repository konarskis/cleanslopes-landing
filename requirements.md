# CleanSlopes — Zero‑Waste Remote Mulching for Cyprus

## Why Choose CleanSlopes?
Cyprus landscapes are rugged, regulations are strict and tourism guests are sensitive to noise and fumes. CleanSlopes keeps your slopes, banks and roughs immaculate **without a single bag of waste, a drop of fuel or a municipal fine**. Our ultra‑light, battery‑electric remote mulchers turn knee‑high weeds into a soil‑building mulch layer while you keep business running and guests relaxing.
